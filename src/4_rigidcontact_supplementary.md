# 补充： Cross Product as a Matrix Product    

We can convert the cross product \\(\mathbf{r}\times\\) into a matrix product \\(\mathbf{r}^*\\).    

![](./assets/04-23.png)    


> &#x2705; \\(\mathbf{r}^*\\) 是 \\(\mathbf{r}\\) 的 cross matrix.   
目的：用矩阵形式代替叉乘形式，方便公式化简
